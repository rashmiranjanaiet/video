# Privacy Policy

## Privacy Statement

NovaLink AI ("we", "us", "our") operates the NovaLink AI service. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## Information Collection and Use

### Types of Data Collected

**Personal Data:**
- Email address
- Username
- Profile information (bio, interests, preferences)
- Audio/video data during calls (not stored permanently)
- Chat messages
- Location data (if provided)
- Device information

**Usage Data:**
- Pages visited
- Features used
- Time and duration of activities
- Browser type and version
- IP address
- Cookies

### How We Use Your Data

1. **To Provide Service**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Facilitate video/voice calls
   - Process messages
   - Connect you with other users

2. **To Improve Service**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Develop new features
   - Improve performance
   - Personalize experience

3. **For Safety & Security**
   - Prevent fraud
   - Enforce terms of service
   - Protect against abuse
   - Comply with legal obligations

4. **To Communicate**
   - Send service updates
   - Respond to support requests
   - Send promotional materials (with consent)

## Data Protection

### Security Measures
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Secure authentication (JWT tokens)
- Regular security audits
- Limited access to personal data

### Retention Policy
- User profile data: Retained until account deletion
- Chat messages: Retained for 30 days
- Call recordings: Not stored
- Activity logs: Retained for 90 days
- Deleted data: Permanently removed within 30 days

## Your Rights

### You Have the Right To:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Restrict processing
- Export your data
- Object to processing
- Lodge complaints

### Exercising Your Rights
- Contact us at privacy@novalink.ai
- Provide necessary identification
- Response within 30 days
